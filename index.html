◈ Jigsaw Sudoku
The nine 3×3 boxes are replaced with irregular jigsaw-shaped regions. Fill each row, column, and region with 1–9.
✕ Killer Sudoku
Cells are grouped into dashed cages, each showing a target sum. Digits within a cage must be unique and add up to that total.
→ Arrow Sudoku
Digits along each arrow must sum to the number in the circle at the arrow's base. Standard Sudoku rules also apply.
